# Lily AI Pricing Model

## Overview

This document outlines the pricing structure for Lily AI, a comprehensive AI-powered research and review platform designed for students. The pricing model is designed to provide clear value while maintaining sustainable economics.

## Subscription Tiers

### Free Tier

- **Price**: £0 (Free)
- **What you get**:
  - Access to 3 complete example research packs (Arts, Humanities, Science)
  - 1 example paper review
  - All examples are fixed topics (non-customizable)
- **Purpose**: Product demonstration only
- **Limitations**: No custom papers or reviews, just samples
- **Value**: Shows the full capabilities of Lily AI without providing directly usable content for coursework

### Premium Subscription

- **Price**: £20/month
- **What you get**: 10 total credits per month
- **Credit usage**:
  - 1 credit = 1 research pack (20-22K words)
  - 1 credit = 1 paper review
- **Features**:
  - Full customization of topics
  - Complete research packs with all sections
  - Comprehensive paper reviews with Lily callouts
  - Flexible allocation between research and review
- **Value**: £2 per substantial interaction (research pack or review)

## Add-on Credit Packs

For Premium subscribers who need additional credits:

- **5 additional credits**: £8.99 (£1.80 per credit)
- **10 additional credits**: £15.99 (£1.60 per credit)
- **25 additional credits**: £34.99 (£1.40 per credit)

Credits can be used for either research packs or paper reviews, providing maximum flexibility.

## Research Pack Details

Each research pack includes:

- 20-22K words of comprehensive content
- Topic Analysis
- Methodological Approaches
- Key Arguments with Evidence
- Citations and Resources
- Lily Callouts (contextual insights)
- Diagrams and Visual Aids (where appropriate)
- Professional Formatting

## Paper Review Details

Each paper review includes:

- Comprehensive critical analysis
- Lily Callouts with improvement suggestions
- Structure and flow assessment
- Argument strength evaluation
- Citation and evidence review
- Language and style feedback
- Suggested revisions

## Rationale

This pricing model:

1. **Demonstrates Value**: Free tier shows full product capability without enabling abuse
2. **Provides Flexibility**: Unified credit system allows students to allocate resources based on their needs
3. **Maintains Quality**: Pricing supports the delivery of high-quality content
4. **Offers Scalability**: Add-on packs provide cost-effective options for higher usage
5. **Aligns with Student Workflow**: Supports the complete academic process from research to review

## Implementation Notes

- Credits expire at the end of each billing cycle
- Subscription auto-renews monthly unless cancelled
- Payment processing via Stripe
- Academic email verification recommended but not required
