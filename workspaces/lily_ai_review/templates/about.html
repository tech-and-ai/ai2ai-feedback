About Lily AI

Our journey began at ResearchAssistant.uk with a vision to produce deep, comprehensive research that could compete with established players in the academic assistance space. However, as we evolved, we realized there was a more meaningful path forward.

We listened carefully to students and researchers, understanding their true needs went beyond just receiving finished research papers. They needed guidance, suggestions, and a partner in their learning journey. This insight led to the birth of Lily AI.

Lily AI represents our commitment to providing not just answers, but a complete learning experience. Unlike other providers who simply deliver finished work, we focus on deep research capabilities combined with personalized advice and suggestions that help improve your academic journey.

Our platform is designed to be an assistant that grows with you, offering insights that enhance your understanding rather than replacing your critical thinking. We believe in empowering students and researchers with tools that complement their abilities and help them achieve their full potential.

Our Values

Academic Integrity

We believe in upholding the highest standards of academic integrity. Our AI is designed to assist research, not replace critical thinking or original work. All content generated includes proper citations and is designed to be a starting point for deeper exploration.

Accessibility

We're committed to making high-quality research accessible to everyone, regardless of background or resources. Our tiered pricing model ensures that students and researchers at all levels can benefit from our platform.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible with AI in academic research. Our team is dedicated to implementing the latest advancements in natural language processing and machine learning to improve our service.

User Privacy

We respect your privacy and handle your data with the utmost care. Your research topics and personal information are never shared with third parties, and we implement robust security measures to protect your data.
