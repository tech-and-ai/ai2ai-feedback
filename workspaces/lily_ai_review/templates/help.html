How to Use Lily AI

Creating a Research Paper

  1. Click on New Research Paper from your dashboard
  2. Enter your research topic in the main field
  3. Add specific questions or areas you want the AI to focus on
  4. Click Generate Research to start the process
  5. Wait while our AI analyzes your topic and creates a comprehensive research pack
  6. Once complete, you'll be redirected to view your new research paper

Managing Your Papers

  • Access all your papers from the My Papers section
  • View, download, or share your research papers
  • Each paper includes a comprehensive analysis, key findings, and references
  • You can download your papers in PDF format for offline use

Subscription and Paper Credits

  • Free users have access to sample papers only
  • Premium users have a monthly allocation of paper credits
  • You can purchase additional paper credits from the subscription page
  • Your current subscription status and remaining papers are displayed on your dashboard

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the research?

Lily AI uses advanced AI models to generate research based on the latest available information. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend verifying critical information from primary sources.

Can I edit my research after it's generated?

Currently, direct editing within the platform is not available. However, you can download your research in PDF format and edit it using your preferred document editor.

How many papers can I create?

This depends on your subscription tier. Free users can access sample papers only, while Premium users have a monthly allocation of paper credits.

What if I run out of paper credits?

You can purchase additional paper credits from the subscription page or upgrade your Premium subscription for more credits.

When will Review Packs be available?

We're working hard to launch Review Packs soon! This exciting feature will allow you to upload your assignments for AI-powered feedback, citation suggestions, and improvements. Stay tuned for announcements on your dashboard.
