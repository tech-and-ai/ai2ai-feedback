Using Research Packs Ethically

Our research packs are designed to support your academic journey, not replace your own critical thinking and analysis. Here's how to use them ethically:

Recommended Uses:

  • As a starting point for your own research
  • To understand complex topics quickly
  • To discover relevant sources and citations
  • For background information on unfamiliar topics

Not Recommended:

  • Submitting as your own work without modification
  • Using without verifying sources and facts
  • Relying solely on our research without critical thinking

Always follow your institution's academic integrity policies. Our service is designed to support your learning, not replace it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive a research pack?

Your purchased credits never expire, but you need an active premium subscription to use them. If your subscription ends, your credits will be saved and become available again when you reactivate your premium subscription.

If you cancel your premium subscription, you'll keep any purchased credits, but you won't be able to use them until you resubscribe. Your credits will be waiting for you when you return to premium status.

What types of topics can I get research packs for?

We cover a wide range of topics across various disciplines. From humanities to sciences, we have you covered! Just let us know your specific topic or research question.

How does your research process work?

Our AI research engine accesses academic databases and scholarly sources to gather information on your topic. It analyzes 40+ relevant academic papers, synthesizes the information, and structures it into a comprehensive research pack with proper citations. Each pack undergoes automated fact-checking to ensure accuracy and originality.

Is using your service considered plagiarism?

Our research packs are meant to be used as reference materials and starting points for your own work. Using them as intended—to inform your research, understand complex topics, and discover relevant sources—is not plagiarism. However, submitting our content as your own work without proper attribution or significant modification would violate academic integrity policies at most institutions. We encourage ethical use of our service.

We offer 24/7 email support for all users. Premium subscribers also have access to live chat support during business hours (9am-6pm UK time).
