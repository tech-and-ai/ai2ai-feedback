About Us

The Gilded Comb is a haven of classic elegance and expert hairstyling. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing you with personalized service and timeless styles that enhance your natural beauty. We believe in creating a relaxing and rejuvenating experience for every client.

Services

  • Classic Cuts & Styles: From bobs to updos, we master the classics.
  • Traditional Coloring: Single process, highlights, and grey coverage.
  • Perms & Texturizing: Adding body and bounce with time-tested techniques.
  • Scalp Treatments: Rejuvenating treatments for healthy hair growth.
  • Special Occasion Styling: Perfect for weddings, galas, and more.

Our History

Established in 1950, The Gilded Comb has been a cornerstone of the community for generations. Our stylists are highly trained and passionate about their craft. We use only the finest products to ensure the health and beauty of your hair. We pride ourselves on our commitment to providing exceptional service and creating a welcoming atmosphere for all.
